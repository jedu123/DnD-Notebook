====== College of Charity ======
After every war, people are holding to their lives, widows and orphans are mourning, parents that will never see their children again are fighting the sorrow. The College of Charity tries to ease their suffering by saving the loved ones on the battlefield and bringing peace to the heart of the wounded and sick. Their presence is a beacon of hope, turning tears to laughs and pain to joy.

==== Bonus Proficiencies (Lv. 3) ====
At joining this college and partaking their gatherings, you gain proficiency and expertise with Medicine.

==== Healing Hand (Lv. 3) ====
You can aid your allies when they are injured. As a reaction, you can expend one of your uses of Bardic Inspiration, rolling a Bardic Inspiration die and adding the number rolled plus your proficiency modifier, and adding that to their health of an ally within 60ft.

==== Divine Grace (Lv. 6) ====
The divine has noted your peerless charity, and they have granted you tools to aid in your pursuit. You learn the spells //Mass Healing Word, Revivify, //and //Sanctuary.// These spells count as bard spells for you but don't count against the number of bard spells you know.

==== Song of Hope (Lv. 14) ====
You are the light that brings the dying back to life. You can use an action on your turn to use and instrument or your voice to bolster your allies. Until the beginning of your next turn, allies within 60ft of you have Advantage on every saving throws, including Death Saves, and immunity to fear effects. For up to one minute, you can use an action to keep this effect up. Once you use this feature, you cannot use it again until you complete a long rest.
