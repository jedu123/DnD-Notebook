====== Halfling ======


==== Ability Score Increase ====
Dex+2

==== Age ====
Halflings reach adulthood at the age of 20 and generally live into the middle of their third century.

==== Alignment ====
Most halflings are lawful good. As a rule, they are good-hearted and kind, hate to see others in pain, and have no tolerance for oppression. They are also very orderly and traditional, leaning heavily on the support of their community and the comfort of their old ways.

==== Size ====
Halflings average about 1 meter tall and weigh about 20 kilograms. Your size is Small.

==== Speed ====
Your base walking speed is 25 feet.

==== Lucky ====
Fortune favours the bold. When you roll a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll.

==== Brave ====
A hero's resolve must be stronger than their steel. You have Advantage on saving throws against being frightened.

==== Halfling Nimbleness ====
Being small has its advantages. You can move through the space of any creature that is of a size larger than yours.

==== Small Folk Defender ====
You have advantage on any Charisma (Persuasion) check done with other races from the Small Folk.

==== Languages ====
You can speak, read, and write Ezor and Cainnt. They write very little, so they don't have a rich body of literature. Their oral tradition, however, is robust. Almost all halflings speak Ezor to converse with the people in whose lands they dwell or through which they are travelling.

==== Subraces ====

=== Luathcas (Lightfoot) ===
As a luathcas, you can easily hide from notice, even using other people as cover. You're inclined to be affable and get along well with others. In the Aerin, they have spread the farthest and thus are the most common variety. These are more prone to wanderlust than other halflings, and often dwell alongside other races or take up a nomadic life.

== Ability Score Increase ==
Cha+1

== Naturally Stealthy ==
You can attempt to hide even when you are obscured only by a creature that is at least one size larger than you.

=== Làidir (Stout) ===
As a làidir, you're hardier than average and have some resistance to poison. Some say that stouts have dwarven blood. They are the most used to fill the legions and chapters of Durgora and Gijur.

== Ability Score Increase ==
Con+1

== Stout Resilience ==
You have Advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage.
